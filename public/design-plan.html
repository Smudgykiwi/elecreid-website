Basalte Switches.

Basalte — Sentido, Fibonacci & Chopin

Belgian design. Premium finishes — brushed brass, black, nickel, bronze, and more.

Sentido — capacitive touch, no moving parts, completely flush. Swipe to dim.

Fibonacci — tactile push buttons, LED feedback, same premium finishes.

Chopin — the entry point. Refined push-button, fits standard wall boxes.

Core Eclipse.

Core Eclipse

Australian-designed. Ultra-flush — no visible plate or frame. The switch disappears so your architecture can speak for itself.

Dimmers, switches, and scene controllers. Compatible with most automation platforms.

Control4 Switches.

Control4 — Contemporary & Lux

Purpose-built for the Control4 ecosystem. The tightest possible integration — scenes, device control, and feedback all handled natively.

Contemporary — customisable, backlit buttons. Lux — premium dimmer, clean modern faceplate.

Zetr.

Zetr

Trimless architecture at its purest. No plate, no frame, no visible edge. The switch disappears into the wall.

Recessed into plasterboard during construction. Requires early coordination — the result is worth it.

Ekinex.

Ekinex — 20 Venti & Proxima

Italian design meets smart technology. The broadest selection of finishes available in any switch — metals, painted surfaces, custom RAL colours.

20 Venti — capacitive and mechanical options, integrated sensors. Proxima — sharper bevels, deeper-brushed finishes, machined edge profile.
