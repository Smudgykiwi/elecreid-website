01 · Basalte

Basalte. Belgian engineered.

Basalte is the gold standard. Touch sensitive surfaces, machined housings, twelve finishes.

Sentido. The light switch reinvented. The entire surface is touch sensitive. Single press, long press, multitouch dimming into scenes. RGB feedback, integrated night light, temperature sensor.

Fibonacci. Inspired by the golden ratio. Backlit, laser engraved labels that light up as you approach, so you find it in the dark. Custom text and icons on every button.

Chopin. The architect grade entry to the Basalte family. Refined push button. Fits standard wall boxes.

02 · Core Smart Home

Eclipse. Australian designed.

Core Smart Home's Eclipse range is the new benchmark for Australian designed keypads and thermostats. Switch and thermostat sit in the same family. Built for Australian homes by people who live in them.

Eclipse Thermostat. Super knob control, custom backlit display, air quality measurement. The thermostat that does not need to hide.

Eclipse Switch. One, two and four fold configurations with integrated sensors. Thirteen finishes. Brushed and silky matte, bronze through brass.

03 · Control4

Contemporary and Lux.

Purpose built for the Control4 ecosystem. The tightest possible integration. Scenes, device control and feedback handled natively.

Contemporary. Customisable, backlit buttons.

Lux. Considered dimmer, clean modern faceplate.

04 · Zetr

Zetr. Trimless architecture.

No plate. No frame. No visible edge. The switch disappears into the wall.

Recessed into plasterboard during construction. Requires early coordination. The result is worth it.

05 · Ekinex

Ekinex. Italian precision.

Three Ekinex ranges sit on our spec sheet. 20Venti for the modular essentials. FF Series for the heavier brass framed look. Proxima as the flagship. KNX native across all three.

20Venti. Touch switches, rockers, thermostats and room controllers in a vast palette of brushed metals, painted surfaces and special textures. The right choice for projects that want a modular, future proof, beautifully machined European standard.

Proxima. Sharper bevels, deeper brushed finishes, a machined edge profile that catches light in a way no other keypad manages. Thirteen finishes including antique brass, satin bronze, rose gold and matte stainless.

Choose as many as you like.

Mix and match throughout the home. Most projects use two or three families across different rooms.

03 · Multi-Room Audio

Music in every room.

Three audio levels for each area of the home.

Level 1. Background music. Enough to fill the room without dominating it.

Level 2. Good and clear. Proper listening quality for everyday enjoyment.

Level 3. Pumping. Full range, high fidelity sound for when you want to feel it.

Speakers visible or invisible. Invisible speakers are built into the ceiling or wall and plastered over. They disappear. Priced higher. The result is stunning.
